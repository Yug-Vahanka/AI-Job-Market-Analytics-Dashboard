# 📊 AI Job Market Analytics Dashboard

## 🚀 Project Overview

The **AI Job Market Analytics Dashboard** is an end-to-end Data Analytics project designed to analyze trends in the global Artificial Intelligence job market.

Using a dataset of **10,000+ AI/ML job postings**, this project uncovers hiring trends, salary patterns, skill demand, geographic opportunities, and experience-level requirements across the AI industry.

The project combines **Python, SQL, and Power BI** to transform raw job data into actionable business insights.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Identify the most in-demand AI and Data Science skills.
* Analyze salary trends across job roles and experience levels.
* Discover top hiring locations and companies.
* Compare remote, hybrid, and on-site opportunities.
* Provide data-driven insights for aspiring AI professionals.

### 🔥 Most In-Demand Skills

* Python
* SQL
* Machine Learning
* Deep Learning
* TensorFlow

### 💰 Highest Paying Roles

* AI Architect
* Machine Learning Engineer
* Data Scientist

### 🌍 Hiring Trends

* Significant growth in AI hiring between 2022–2024
* Strong demand for remote AI positions
* Major hiring hubs include Bangalore, Hyderabad, Pune, and Mumbai

### 📈 Experience Trends

* Mid-level roles represent the largest hiring segment
